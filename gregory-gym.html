General Information

Access to this facility is restricted to current UT students and individuals with a current RecSports membership or Day Pass. 

Facility Address

2101 Speedway
Austin, Texas 78712
View Map
Parking Information

Facility Hours

Facility hours vary based on the academic calendar, University holidays, and break periods. 
View Facility Hours

Activities at this Facility

Gregory Gym Offers:

  • Badminton
  • Basketball
  • Cardiovascular Training
  • Fitness Classes
  • Jogging
  • Handball
  • Racquetball
  • Rock Climbing
  • Sauna
  • Squash
  • Steam Room
  • Table Tennis
  • Volleyball
  • Walking
  • Wallyball
  • Weight Training

Features

Gregory Gym Features:

  • 7 basketball or 9 volleyball courts
  • 1/7th-mile indoor jogging track
  • 7 handball/racquetball/wallyball courts
  • 2 international-size squash courts
  • Weight training/cardio room
  • Faculty/staff weight training/cardio room
  • Climbing Wall
  • Multi-purpose/dance rooms
  • Functional training spaces
  • Outdoor Center
  • Steam rooms and saunas
  • Faculty/staff locker room
  • Classroom
  • Exercise studio
  • Group cycling studio
  • Personal training studio
  • Intramural Wall of Fame
